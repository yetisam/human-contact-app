# Human Contact

**"The place to meet real humans."**

A verified contact-exchange platform that enables safe, trust-based connections between real people. Human Contact serves as a bridge — facilitating verified introductions, then encouraging relationships to develop offline.

## What Human Contact Is

- ✅ Safe introduction platform
- ✅ Verified human connection facilitator
- ✅ Contact exchange enabler
- ✅ Launchpad to real friendships

## What Human Contact Is NOT

- ❌ Not a messaging app
- ❌ Not social media
- ❌ Not a dating platform
- ❌ Not designed for long-term in-app engagement

## Tech Stack

- **Mobile & Web:** Flutter (Dart)
- **Backend:** Node.js / Express
- **Database:** PostgreSQL + Prisma ORM
- **Cache:** Redis
- **ID Verification:** Persona
- **Hosting:** Vercel (web) + Railway (backend)

## Project Structure
